Overview

I grew up as an experimentalist (MBE growth + STM measurement), and that work heavily trained my coding: device control, automated data pipelines, and simulation. I’m now intentionally pivoting to AI/ML and large-scale data processing, while formalizing the math and CS foundations behind them. I’m a fast, self-directed learner and enjoy turning messy experimental data into clear, reproducible results.

Previously

  • MBE growth of high-quality films: Fe(Se,Te)/Bi2Te3 (SC/TI), MnTe/Bi2Te3 (altermagnet/TI), and K3C60 (candidate for light-induced superconductivity; Tc ≈ 19 K).
  • STM measurements of thin films: topography & dI/dV, reliable tip conditioning, and automated processing.
  • Wrote an MBE feedback controller; built STM data pipelines; developed a QPI toolkit (JDOS & spin-polarized QPI).

Now

  • Numerical methods & optimization (convex/non-convex), inverse problems, uncertainty modeling.
  • Probability & statistics: estimation, hypothesis testing, Bayesian reasoning, Monte Carlo.
  • Time-series & spectral analysis: FFT/CWT, AR/ARIMA, state-space models, system identification.
  • Machine learning: feature engineering, linear/regularized models, tree/boosting, model selection.
  • Reproducible, config-driven pipelines; robust logging; device interfacing and automation.

Currently Studying

  • Linear algebra, numerical optimization, probability & statistics, stochastic processes.
  • Data structures & algorithms, software design/testing, scalable data handling.
