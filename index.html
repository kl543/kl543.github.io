About

I am a Physics PhD student at UIUC. My background is experimental: MBE growth of high-quality thin films and STM measurements (topography, dI/dV, and reliable tip conditioning). As an undergraduate I spent about six months on STM-based experimental research, and later more than a year on computational work modeling Rydberg-atom dynamics. I am now moving toward computational physics and data-centric methods, with a focus on numerics, optimization, statistics, signal processing, and clean, reproducible software.
