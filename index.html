About

I am a Physics PhD student at UIUC. My background is experimental: MBE growth of high-quality thin films and STM measurements (topography, dI/dV, and reliable tip conditioning). As an undergraduate, I spent ~6 months doing STM work at UC Berkeley and later >1 year on computational research modeling Rydberg-atom dynamics. I am now moving toward computational physics and data-centric methods with a focus on numerics, optimization, statistics, signal processing, and clean, reproducible software.
